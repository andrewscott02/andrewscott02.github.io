Overkill

Play Overkill View Project Details

Overkill is a turn-based game inspired by the settings of Doom and XCOM. But unlike those games, you play as the villains. Lead a raiding party of demons to take over the Earth for the forces of evil in this turn-based RPG with a twist.

In Overkill, characters can only die when they take damage that exceeds their armour, otherwise it has no effect. Use actions to increase the damage you can deal or decrease their armour first before unleashing an attack that can one-shot them.
